Groups draw a bracket below the chart axis spanning the selected bars. Each group shows a label and the net sum of its bars. Anchors and subtotals are excluded from the group sum.

Two columns: Label then Value. Use "anchor" or "total" in the value column to set keyword anchors. Negatives become red bars automatically.

If your last row is an anchor with a real number (e.g. "FY24 Revenue 10130"), the chart auto-switches to Tie-Out mode and inserts a plug bar. If it's a keyword anchor, Free-Form mode is used.
